# Better Expressions

## You are welcome

- No worries!
- Any time!
- That's alright!
- It was nothing!
- Not a problem!
- It's a pleasure!

## Me Too

- Positive: So _aux_ I.
- Negative: Neither _aux_ I.

Aux is one of: [am | can | would | will | do] depending on the sentence.

So do I, So would I, Neither did I.

## Could you repeat?

- Sorry
- I didn't (quite) (get | catch) that
- Sorry, what was that?

## Goodbye

- Cheers
- See you later
- Right, I'm off
